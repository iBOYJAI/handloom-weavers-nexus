Returns & Exchanges

Our Policy

We want you to love your handloom purchase. If you are not entirely satisfied, we are here to help. You have 7 calendar days to return an item from the date you received it.

Conditions for Return

  • The item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it.
  • The item must be in its original packaging with all tags intact.
  • Please note that slight irregularities in weave or color are characteristics of handloom and are not considered defects.

How to Initiate a Return

To initiate a return, please email us at returns@handloomnexus.com with your order number and images of the product. Once approved, we will arrange for a pickup.
