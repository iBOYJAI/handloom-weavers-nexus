Shipping Policy

Direct from the Loom

Your order is shipped directly from the weaver's location to your doorstep. This ensures authenticity and supports the local ecosystem. Most orders are processed within 48 hours.

Delivery Times

  • Domestic (India): 5-9 business days.
  • International: 12-18 business days.

Shipping Charges

We offer free shipping on orders above ₹2,000 within India. For orders below this amount, a flat fee of ₹150 applies. International shipping is calculated at checkout based on location and weight.
