Our Direct-to-Weaver Model

By removing intermediaries, we ensure that over 85% of the final purchase price goes directly to the weaver's family. This has resulted in a 40% increase in average monthly income for our partner artisans compared to traditional market channels.

Preserving Cultural Heritage

We focus on reviving rare weaving techniques that were bordering on extinction. From the complex Jamdani of Bengal to the intricate Patola of Gujarat, we help find a modern market for ancient arts.
