Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know the sarees are authentic?
Every saree on Handloom Nexus is uploaded directly by registered artisan weavers. Many come with regional certifications (like Handloom Mark or Silk Mark) noted in the description.
How long does shipping take?
Since items are shipped directly from the weaver's location, delivery typically takes 5-9 business days depending on your region.
Can I return a saree?
Yes, we have a 7-day return policy for unused products in their original packaging. Please see our Returns & Exchanges page for full details.
How can I support a specific weaver?
You can follow a weaver's profile, read their stories, and purchase products directly from their collections. Share their stories to help them reach a wider audience!
